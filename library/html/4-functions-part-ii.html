1 - LEADING FUNCTION

[Table content]
[Image content]

Ego, 4D and Assertive

Sets why we do what we do

Assumes others view the world similarly

Takes precedence, decides our priorities

Master of the psyche, calls the shots

All slides are copyrighted by Jack Oliver Aaron of the World Socionics Society

2 - CREATIVE FUNCTION

[Table content]
[Image content]

Ego, 3D and Collaborative

Implements the Leading’s program

Gives it a distinct flavour or focus

Adapts and adjusts itself without fuss

Helpful sidekick, follows orders with skill

3 - ROLE FUNCTION

[Table content]
[Image content]

Super-Ego, 2D and Reluctant

The “act” we feel made to put on

Uncomfortable to use, crude and inconsistent

Responds to risks and external expectations

Fails if supported by Vulnerable

4 - VULNERABLE FUNCTION

[Table content]
[Image content]

Super-Ego, 1D and Intransigent

The blind spot we cannot use properly

Beyond our understanding or appreciation

Totally rejected and avoided

Refuses to support the Role

All slides are copyrighted by Jack Oliver Aaron of the World Socionics Society

5 - SUGGESTIVE FUNCTION

[Table content]
[Image content]

Super-Id, 1D and Collaborative

Unfulfilled needs we know not how to satisfy

Naturally complements the Leading function

Lacks confidence, seeks guidance from others

Where true pain and vulnerability can be felt

All slides are copyrighted by Jack Oliver Aaron of the World Socionics Society

6 - MOBILISING FUNCTION

[Table content]
[Image content]

Super-Id, 2D and Assertive

Aspirations and areas of personal growth

How we would like to be

Must prove itself to support the Suggestive

Often overconfident and can backfire

All slides are copyrighted by Jack Oliver Aaron of the World Socionics Society

7 - IGNORING FUNCTION

[Table content]
[Image content]

Id, 3D and Intransigent

Suppressed by Leading, kept in a cage

Only used when necessary to the 1.

Seen as the “backward” or “wrong” thing to do

Expertly unused until let out the cage

All slides are copyrighted by Jack Oliver Aaron of the World Socionics Society

[Table content]
[Image content]

Id, 4D and Reluctant

Highly active, often feeding 2

As strong as Leading but not taken seriously

Taken entirely for granted, used to relax

Returned to when confidence is low
