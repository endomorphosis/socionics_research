GAMMA VALUES

All Valued Elements are Repulsive.

Thinking independently and acting autonomously

Identifying and avoiding present threats and future risks

Being critical and resisting stupid ideas or evil intentions

Exercising one’s own competence and initiative for personal success

All slides are copyrighted by Jack Oliver Aaron of the World Socionics Society

GAMMA TABOOS

All Neglected Elements are Attractive.

Falling to peer pressure rather than thinking for oneself

Brushing over real problems with superficial positivity

Wasting time with useless ideas that only make sense in theory

Embracing mediocrity, being lazy, and lacking personal ambition
